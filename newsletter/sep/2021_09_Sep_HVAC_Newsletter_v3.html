Great Activities from MENA
Recently, we have heard many news from Middle East. Many successful project cases and
other activities have been shared through Newsletters of HVAC and WindFree™. Hopefully,
their experiences could inspire other regions and improve together. This time, two great
activities have been shared: MENA SAC STEP (Sales Training & Empowerment Program) and
Consultants & Contractors Seminar hosted by SGE & MEDAO in Ras Al Khaimah.
MENA SAC STEP

STEP is a MEDAO initiative of E-learning & competition program for SAC partners
across MENA. It is aimed to promote E-learning culture, enhance product
knowledge & improve sales skills of partners. The E-learning sessions include
courses on product features, PODs and introduction to DVM Pro2.0.

The participants should pass 4 steps:
E-learning, online test, project design competition & sales presentation
competition.

2021 STEP resulted in an enormous 444% growth in E-learning in 1 H ’21. These
trainings enabled partners to design optimized systems, which will help them to
win strategic BOs.

As a part of appreciating the efforts from Subsidiaries for enrolling maximum
users in STEP, an Award was given to the Subsidiary that enrolled most
participants in STEP ’21. SELV SAC team won this award.

Consultants, Contractors Seminar- RAK
SGE & MEDAO co-hosted ‘Consultants & Contractors Seminar’ in Waldorf Astoria Hotel, Ras Al Khaimah “RAK”, UAE on 24th
June ‘21. This was the first off-line event, benefitting from the relaxation of pandemic regulations by authorities. RAK is
the 4th largest Emirate in UAE & is one of the major adventure sport and tourist destinations in GCC.

The event was organized in association with UAE Disty
Faisal Jassim (FJ), to promote the innovative SAC products
and solutions to the major consultants & contractors.

The event was attended by 18 participants representing
RAK construction industry & real estate sector.

SGE delivered a technical presentation focusing on 360
Cassette and WindFree™.

Potential BOs of value $5.6M were generated from the
event. Inspired by the success of this event, SGE is planning
for two more off-line events in ‘21. 2H in UAE.
