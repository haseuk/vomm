Q 04

What is the benefit
of a 90cc compressor?

"A large compression chamber is
the basis of a large displacement volume, which ensures the system performs more effectively and reliably in any climate conditions."

In an air conditioning system, the refrigerant is circulated among outdoor units and indoor units by a compressor, exchanging heat to cool or heat the air. The larger the capacity of the compressor the more refrigerant it can pump out per revolution. So, a large capacity is essential to create a large displacement volume of refrigerant.

With a 90cc compression chamber, combining with an increased rotary power, the Samsung Advanced Flash Injection Compressor provides the world's largest capacity. It means it can effectively and reliably cool or heat many more and much larger spaces, even in extreme weather conditions. In addition, it also consumes less electricity, so you can stay comfortable all year round without worrying about electricity bills.
