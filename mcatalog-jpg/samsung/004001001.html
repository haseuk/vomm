Enlarged Heat Exchanger

The Samsung DVM S2 has an enlarged heat exchanger that is capable of transferring much more heat at once. Its heat transfer area is up to 36.2% larger* to quickly exchange heat. As a result, it consumes less energy to achieve the same cooling and heating performance.

* Based on Samsung's measurements, comparing 33.6kW models of a DVM S2 and a conventional outdoor unit.
