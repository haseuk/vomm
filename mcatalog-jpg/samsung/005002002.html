Q 02

What
is the energy efficiency of the Samsung DVM S2 like? Is there any official evidence to prove it?

"The DVM S2 is proven to improve seasonal energy efficiency by around 11% across a range of capacities on average, based on the Eurovent testing rule."

Based on testing in accordance with the Eurovent testing rule, conducted at the end of December 2020, the Seasonal
Energy Efficiency Ratio (SEER) of the Samsung DVM S2 is much higher than other brands, across all capacity models. The maximum gap was up to 25% higher, and around 11% higher on average*.

* Based on testing in accordance with the Eurovent testing rule, at the end of December 2020. Tested on 22.4~56kW models of the Samsung DVM S2 compared to the same capacities of another brand, using ducted type indoor units.
