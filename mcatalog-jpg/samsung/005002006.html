Q 06

When a normal pipe is replaced with a slimmer pipe in a liquid refrigerant line, does it affect the system’s performance?

"No, downsizing of a liquid pipe doesn't affect the performance of the air conditioning, including the displacement volume."

Based on internal testing in Samsung's Test Lab, downsizing the main pipe for the liquid refrigerant has a very minor impact on the cooling and heating capacity, which is still within the allowable range according to the relevant ISO standard*. So, it has virtually no effect, and there is no need to redesign the system capacity to compensate for any change in performance.
