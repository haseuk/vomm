Q 03

What are the keys to achieving much higher energy efficiency?

"Every component and operational process of the DVM S2 incorporates various innovative technologies, including AI, to maximize efficiency."

The enlarged Heat Exchanger and its optimized refrigerant path maximize the transfer rate while minimizing the loss of energy. An aerodynamic Multi-serration Fan generates more air flow, but consumes less electricity. The high- efficiency IGBT reduces the loss of conducted electricity. And many other parts also contribute to saving energy.

At the same time, Active AI Pressure Control adjusts the compression pressure automatically to suit the conditions of each installation site and to reduce energy usage.

In addition, the revolutionary Samsung Advanced Flash Injection Compressor not only delivers a superior and reliable heating performance at low temperature, but it also operates in the most efficient way. Its Optimal Discharge Superheat (DSH) Control functionality optimally adjusts the degree of superheat, depending on the heating load and external temperature, to improve performance and efficiency.
