Q 01

What are the key reasons my customer should buy the Samsung DVM S2?

"Remember EASILy, then you'll easily explain its key benefits
- Efficiency, AI, Servicing, Installation and Line-up."

1. Efficiency

"Top-class seasonal energy efficiency" The DVM S2 has been radically redesigned to reduce the operating costs with unique technologies that are significantly more efficient. As a result, it is proven to improve seasonal energy efficiency by around 11% across a range of capacities*, on average.

2. Artificial Intelligence (AI)

"AI innovation" By learning usage patterns from recent operations and the surrounding conditions, the DVM S2 proactively creates, optimizes and maintains the most comfortable environment, while reducing energy consumption.

3. Servicing & Installation

"Convenient and cost-effective installation
and servicing" The DVM S2 requires less refrigerant when using a slimmer liquid pipe, which also means you can design the entire system more flexibly and install it with less effort and lower costs. And On-Device Inverter Check™ and S-converter simplify the process of checking errors, so the servicing is easier and takes less time.

4. Line-up

"Wide and varied line-up of indoor units" The DVM S2 is perfectly compatible with almost all kinds of Samsung indoor units, including the state-of-the-art WindFree™ air conditioners and 360 Cassette. In particular, it controls the compressor more precisely, so it enhances their ability to deliver WindFree™ comfort in the optimal way.

* Based on testing in accordance with the Eurovent testing rule, at the end of December 2020. Tested on 22.4~56kW models of the Samsung DVM S2 compared to the same capacities of another brand, using ducted type indoor units.
