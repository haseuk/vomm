Reliability

Designed to perform in freezing conditions

The Samsung DVM S2 is capable of delivering a high heating capacity at extremely low ambient temperatures in a reliable and energy-efficient way. Its base design helps drain condensed water fast, and a base heater melts any ice that forms inside.

Upgraded Base Design

During the heating operation in cold weather, the defrost cycle used to remove ice on a heat exchanger can cause water to condense on it. The DVM S2 has a patented base design with drainage holes that cover a 6.6 times larger area, so it drains condensed water much faster to prevent it from freezing inside the cabinet.
