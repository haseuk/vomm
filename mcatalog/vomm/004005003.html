On-Device Inverter Check™

The DVM S2 has an Inverter PBA (Printed Board Assembly) with a one-touch button to simply check errors on the device. Without having to remove the entire front cover, it’s easy to access this button through a small opening in the Display Window. So, it reduces the service time and effort as it eliminates the need for extra tools and simplifies the service process.
